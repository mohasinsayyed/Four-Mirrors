# Four Mirrors

**The only kundli app that tells you the truth while it reads you.**

Four traditions — Jyotish, Numerology (Chaldean + Pythagorean), Western astrology, and Faal — computed honestly, compared openly, and explained so simply a school kid can follow every word.

**Live app:** open `index.html` in any browser, or visit the GitHub Pages URL once enabled.

## What it does

- **Jyotish:** lagna, rashi chart, nakshatra & pada, full Vimshottari dasha timeline (past / present / future framed honestly), navamsa (D9), all 12 life areas with planetary aspects (drishti), intimacy, spiritual life, character & values, job-vs-business leaning
- - **People in your life:** spouse, children, parents, in-laws, friends, colleagues, manager, increment, rise & fall — each with Do / Don't / Most expected / Most unexpected
  - - **Marriage matching:** full 36-point Guna Milan (Ashtakoota) with Mangal dosha check — consent-gated
    - - **Numerology:** name, birth, destiny, mobile & vehicle numbers — both systems shown, contradictions displayed, never hidden
      - - **Western:** all ten planets, Midheaven, weekday lord
        - - **Faal:** original reflective verses, honestly labeled
          - - **The Four Mirrors Speak Together:** one combined output — convergences counted, contradictions named
            - - **Educational module:** how "guaranteed hit" predictions work, in five lessons
              - - **Full report download** — honesty panels included by design
               
                - ## What makes it different
               
                - Every section carries an honesty panel: *what tradition says / what it cannot tell you / what actually decides this.* The engine's ±1° accuracy is disclosed on-screen, boundary cases are flagged, classical table variations are admitted, and the app computes **your own chart only** — it generates no verdicts on anyone's character or fidelity, and answers no question about another person's body or choices.
               
                - Validated against professional ephemeris output: all ten planets within 0.3°.
               
                - ## Privacy
               
                - Everything computes on your device. Nothing is uploaded, stored, or tracked.
               
                - ## Hosting (recommended)
               
                - Enable **GitHub Pages** (Settings → Pages → Deploy from branch → main → root). The app then runs at a tap for anyone with the link — no downloads, no Drive-preview problems.
