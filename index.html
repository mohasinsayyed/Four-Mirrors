Mirror 1 · Jyotish (Vedic tradition)Birth chart essentials

Rashi = the zodiac sign a planet sits in (sidereal — measured against the actual stars). Lagna = the sign rising on the eastern horizon at birth; tradition treats it as the "self". Nakshatra = one of 27 lunar mansions; the Moon's nakshatra drives the dasha clock.

Jyotish · navamsa (D9) — नवांशThe inside-strength chart, explained simply

Imagine each sign as a room, and now cut every room into 9 small slices of 3°20′ each. The slice a planet stands in makes a second, finer chart — the navamsa. Kids' version: the main chart (D1) is how strong a player looks; the navamsa (D9) is how strong they play in the finals. Tradition uses it mostly for marriage and a planet's true inner strength. A planet in the same sign in both charts (called vargottama) is read as especially steady.

Jyotish · timingVimshottari dasha — the tradition's life chapters

Mahadasha = a major planetary period (chapters of life, 6–20 years each). Antardasha = the sub-chapter inside it. The full cycle is 120 years, started by the Moon's position at birth.

Jyotish · the 12 life areas (bhavas)Life, money, relations & more — explained simply

Bhava (house) = tradition divides life into 12 areas, like 12 rooms of a house. Each room gets a sign, a caretaker planet (its "lord"), and sometimes planets living inside it. Below, every room is explained in plain words.

Jyotish · spiritual lifeThe inner journey — what tradition reads, with respect

Tradition reads the spiritual side through Ketu (the moksha planet — letting go), Jupiter (the guru — wisdom & guidance), the 5th room (merit carried in), the 9th room (dharma) and the 12th room (liberation & retreat).
A line this tool will not cross This tool will never declare any person "ethical" or "unethical", honest or dishonest, from a chart. No birth detail can measure character — only repeated actions can. A verdict like that, printed by software, would be used against real people: friends, partners, employees, in-laws. If you want to judge someone's honesty, watch what they do over twelve months. That reading is free and it is accurate.

Jyotish · workJob or business — tradition's leaning

The classic question. Tradition reads it from the 10th room (career), its caretaker, and the strength of Mercury (trade), Saturn (service) and Mars (independent drive). It gives a leaning, never a command.

Mirror 3 · Western astrology (tropical)Sun, Moon & Rising — the other way of reading the same sky

Why the signs differ from Jyotish: Western astrology measures from the seasons (tropical); Jyotish measures from the stars (sidereal). The two rulers are ~24° apart today — so the same planet at the same moment often lands in different signs. Both cannot be describing the same truth; watch the comparison table below.

Fidelity & trustThe section every app dodges — handled straight

What this app will not compute No chart detects or predicts an affair — yours or anyone's. Classical texts do speculate from an "afflicted 7th house," and real marriages have been wrecked by accusations built on exactly that speculation, sometimes by one astrologer's sentence. This tool will not generate that sentence in either direction: not "your spouse will stray" (a character verdict on a real person from fabricated evidence) and not "you are destined to stray" (a permission slip pretending to be fate — nothing is written; choices are choices).

What actually protects a marriage: affection expressed in ordinary weeks, conflicts repaired rather than buried, and needs spoken before they go looking for other doors. What to do with real suspicion: one direct, private, kind conversation — and if trust is genuinely broken, a counsellor, not a chart. What to do with an astrologer's warning about your spouse: nothing. A stranger's speculation is not evidence about the person who shares your home.
